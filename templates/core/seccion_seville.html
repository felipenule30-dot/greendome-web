Cannabis Club Seville

Green Dome — Seville's Premier Private Cannabis Social Club

Green Dome is a private, non-profit cannabis social club located in the heart of Seville, in the vibrant Nervión neighbourhood. Founded in November 2025, we are a members-only association built around quality, community and responsible consumption.

Unlike a cannabis store or dispensary, Green Dome operates exclusively as a private social club under Spanish law. Our products come from our own cultivation — 100% controlled, premium quality at every step.

Looking for a cannabis club in Seville? Green Dome is the private cannabis social club Seville members trust. We are a cannabis club Spain reference — not a cannabis store, but a private non-profit association in Nervión, Seville.
